What We Do

Our Goal

Our goal is to educate and reinforce foreign language skills in a debate context. Most Americans are monolingual because they never had the opportunity to practice language skills outside of class. We want to change that by fostering a debate program that integrates the foreign language curriculum. We encourage non-native speakers to step outside of their comfort zone and utilize the skills they learn in class in these debates in order to reinforce the languages themselves.
