Get Involved

Donate

You can donate, either online or by check, to support our cause. All proceeds go towards:


Funding and organizing debates


Financial assistance to schools and students in need


Researching further debate topics

Volunteer

You can become a member of the Association and perform the following tasks as a volunteer:


Assist training students for all debates


Participate as a judge


Assist in organizing/cleaning up before and after events

All volunteers will receive mandatory training sessions prior to being able to participate and will be evaluated based on their performance in said training sessions

Requirements to volunteer: Must be 21 years of age and have a level of proficiency in the Spanish Language in order to judge or train students. Must be 16 years of age to help set up/clean up for debates
